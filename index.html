What This Is

A public research system, not just a tool page.

Start with one name, company, username, email, website, claim, or topic. Use structured search paths, AI-assisted organization, and source-grounded review to build a cleaner file.

The goal is not to make research look complicated. The goal is to make it repeatable, explainable, and useful.

Possible Outputs
  • •Research summary
  • •Professional-style report
  • •Timeline or entity map
  • •Slide deck or script outline
  • •Reusable prompt and source checklist
